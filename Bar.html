Taurus

Introduction

The Taurus Cocktail offers a warm embrace of flavors, where the rich, oaky undertones of whiskey mingle with the exuberant sweetness of pineapple. The citrus tang of lemon juice adds a refreshing sharpness, artfully balanced by the velvety caress of simple syrup. With the fragrant flourish of orange zest, this cocktail delivers a comforting yet invigorating tapestry of tastes, embodying the grounded yet indulgent spirit of its zodiac inspiration.

Ingredients

  • 1 oz Whiskey
  • 1.5 oz Pineapple Juice
  • 0.5 oz Lemon Juice
  • 0.5 oz Simple Syrup
  • 1 Orange Zest

White Russian

Introduction

The White Russian cocktail is a velvety indulgence, a luxurious blend of smooth vodka and rich coffee liqueur, mellowed by the creamy caress of heavy cream and whole milk. Each sip is a harmonious balance of strength and sweetness, reminiscent of a decadent dessert that warms the soul. Embrace the comforting embrace of this classic concoction, where the boldness of the spirits is softened by the silky whispers of cream, crafting an experience that's both invigorating and soothing.

Ingredients

  • 0.5 oz Vodka
  • 1 oz Coffee Liqueur
  • 0.5 oz Heavy Cream
  • 0.5 oz Whole Milk

Aegean Sea

Introduction

The Aegean Sea cocktail is an inviting blend that captures the spirit of its namesake's clear blue waters.
